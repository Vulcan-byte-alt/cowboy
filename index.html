Event Overview

Join us for the annual Grand Canyon Classic, a premier team sorting competition in the heart of Arizona. This three-day event brings together the best riders and horses for an unforgettable weekend of competition, camaraderie, and western heritage.

With divisions for all skill levels and substantial prize pools, the Grand Canyon Classic offers something for everyone. Don't miss the chance to compete in one of the West's most prestigious events!

About Western States

Western States Productions was founded by a group of passionate riders who saw the need for premium, well-organized team sorting events across the western United States. What began as a small operation has grown into the premier destination for competitive riders seeking top-tier events with substantial prize pools and unmatched experiences.

Our events are designed with both the competitor and spectator in mind, creating an environment where western heritage meets modern competition. From world-class arenas to meticulously planned schedules, we leave no detail to chance.

  • ✓ Professional arena management with state-of-the-art equipment
  • ✓ Events for all skill levels from novice to professional
  • ✓ Premium cattle selection ensuring fair competition
  • ✓ Substantial prize pools and prestigious championship titles
  • ✓ Family-friendly environments with western entertainment

RIDE LIKE & WYATT

Our signature youth development program nurtures the next generation of western riders through mentorship, training, and competition opportunities. Created in honor of legendary rider Wyatt Johnson, this program has produced some of today's top competitors.

Participants receive professional coaching, entry fee discounts, and exclusive access to workshops throughout the season. Our goal is to preserve western heritage while elevating the sport to new heights.
